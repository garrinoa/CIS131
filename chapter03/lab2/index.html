Welcome to Wild Rescues. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife back into their natural environment. We area a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping injured wildlife.

Our staff provides around-the-clock care for widlife in need. We also provide long-term care for wildlife that cannot be released back into their natural environments.

Will you partner with us? We need caring volunteers to help care for our animals. You can also donate, become a member, or become a sponsor. Contact us today.
